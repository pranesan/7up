About Us

7UP is a popular lemon-lime soda known for its crisp and refreshing taste. Founded in 1929, 7UP has become one of the leading carbonated soft drinks worldwide. Whether you enjoy it on its own or in a cocktail, 7UP is the perfect companion to make every moment a little brighter.

We believe that life is too short for flat moments, and that’s why we bring you the effervescent joy of 7UP, a drink that’s as refreshing as it is lively.
